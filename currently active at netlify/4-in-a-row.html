4-in-a-Row Multiplayer

Classic strategy game for 2-4 players. Connect 4 or 5 tokens in a row to win!

Player 1's Turn

How to Play

  • Players take turns dropping a token into any column
  • The token falls to the lowest available position in that column
  • First player to get 4 in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins
  • With 3+ players, blocking others becomes crucial as you compete against multiple opponents

Game ends in a draw if the board fills up completely.

UPSC (Union Public Service Commission) Updated 2026

Civil Services, Engineering Services, CDS, NDA, and other examinations

New Application Portal: https://upsconline.nic.in Main Website: https://upsc.gov.in
Important Note (2026): UPSC now requires One Time Registration (OTR) for most examinations. After OTR, candidates can apply for multiple exams without re-entering basic details.
Exams Conducted: Civil Services (IAS/IPS/IFS), Engineering Services, Combined Defence Services (CDS), National Defence Academy (NDA), Combined Medical Services (CMS), etc.

SSC (Staff Selection Commission) New Portal

CGL, CHSL, JE, MTS, Stenographer, and other non-technical posts

Official Website: https://ssc.gov.in
Important Update: SSC launched a completely new website for all applications starting in 2024. Fresh registration is required even if you had an account on the old portal.
Regional Websites: SSC has regional websites for different zones - North, South, East, West, etc. Check notifications carefully for regional requirements.

Railway Recruitment Board (RRB)

NTPC, Group D, ALP, Technician, and other railway posts

Note: Candidates must log in to their respective regional RRB websites to apply for exams:

RRB Chandigarh: https://rrbcdg.gov.in RRB Mumbai: https://rrbmumbai.gov.in RRB Chennai: https://rrbchennai.gov.in RRB Kolkata: https://rrbkolkata.gov.in
Important: Always apply through the specific RRB region mentioned in the notification. Applications are region-specific.

Banking Exams (IBPS)

PO, Clerk, SO, RRB Officer, and other banking sector posts

Official Website: https://www.ibps.in
Participating Banks: IBPS conducts common recruitment process for Public Sector Banks (except SBI), Regional Rural Banks (RRBs), and other financial institutions.
Important: SBI (State Bank of India) has its own separate recruitment portal: https://sbi.co.in

National Testing Agency (NTA)

NEET, CUET, UGC NET, JEE Main, and other national level tests

Official Website: https://nta.ac.in
Major Exams:
  • NEET (Medical Entrance)
  • JEE Main (Engineering Entrance)
  • CUET (University Entrance)
  • UGC NET (Research Eligibility)
  • GPAT (Pharmacy Entrance)

National Career Service (NCS)

Centralized portal for government and private job opportunities

Official Portal: https://www.ncs.gov.in
Services Offered:
  • Job search across sectors
  • Career counseling
  • Skill development resources
  • Government job notifications
  • Employer registration

Important Tips for Candidates (2026)

  • Always use official government websites (.gov.in, .nic.in, .ac.in)
  • Beware of phishing websites with similar-looking URLs
  • Keep your registration details and passwords secure
  • Check notification details carefully before applying
  • Download and save your application confirmation
  • Regularly check official websites for updates and corrigendum
